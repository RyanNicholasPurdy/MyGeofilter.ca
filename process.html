OUR PROCESS.

Design

We pride ourselves on constructing flawless designs. Through meticulous studying of market trends, in combination with advanced design pricipals, we craft visuals that are exciting & engaging.

Geofence

After the design process has been completed we select the date, time, & range of your desired Snapchat filter. We can target a small focused area down to 20,000 sq ft, or a much larger reach up to 5,000,000 sq ft.

Launch

The final step in the process is to launch your product. We submit the completed design & geofence to Snapchat for public release approval. Once approved, we send confirmation of when the filter will be live in wild.
